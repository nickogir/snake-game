About Us

Last updated: February 15, 2026

NEON ARCADE is an independent browser games project focused on simple, replayable mini-games that run instantly without downloads.

The goal is to build clear, lightweight game experiences with keyboard-friendly controls, fast restart loops, and consistent performance on desktop and mobile browsers.

Current release: Snake, including wrap-around gameplay, best-score tracking, and live gameplay analytics for iterative product improvements.
