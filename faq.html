FAQ

Last updated: February 13, 2026

How do I start the game?

Press SPACE or any direction key.

How do I restart quickly?

Press SPACE after game over or click the Restart button.

Why do I appear on the opposite border?

This version uses classic wrap-around movement instead of wall collision death.

How is the best score stored?

Best score is stored in your browser local storage on this device.
